About Me

Dream Company

The company I dream of working for is Nike. My obsession with this brand began as a child as a kid who was always involved in sports. My first introduction to athletic activity was through tennis which I played since the age of 6. By the time I was 9, I took up fencing at a local club where I competed with others throughout New York City. When I was 13 getting ready to go to high school, I was advised by my Dad - a track and field coach - to try out his sport. As a freshman, I fell in love with the sport and the competition and thus continued throughout my entire high school career.

Being that I was involved in sports since I was 6, this meant that I've attended a lot of training sessions throughout my life. Thus, I was always dressed in comfortable athletic wear. The Nike swoosh has been a familiar logo throughout my early life but it wasn't until I was older that I later understood the meaning behind the brand.

To me, Nike represents athletic excellence, innovation, and diversity. I'm most impressed with Nike's marketing team who manage to raise goosebumps on my skin whenever they release a new campaign. They often manage to merge the emotion of sport to that within social issues making for powerful statements that facilitate this emotion. I'd love to be involved in a creative process with the Nike team.
